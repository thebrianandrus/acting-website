Short Bio

Brian Andrus was born in Rochester Hills, Michigan. He began acting in musical theater at Brandon High School, appearing as Rum Tum Tugger in Cats and as Curly McLain in Oklahoma!. While attending Michigan State University, he worked on films Dinner in America and Cherry. After relocating to Atlanta, he booked a role in NF's viral music video "CLOUDS". In 2024, Brian appeared in Hometown (TV pilot) and branded content for the Volkswagen I.D. Buzz. He continues to develop his career in Atlanta.
